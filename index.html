Unity in Diversity

Overview

India is a diverse country in South Asia, known for its rich culture, history, languages, and landscapes. It is the seventh-largest country by land area and the most populous democracy in the world.

States of India

India consists of 28 states, each with its own government. Some major states include:

  • Maharashtra
  • Uttar Pradesh
  • Tamil Nadu
  • Rajasthan
  • Karnataka

Union Territories

India has 8 Union Territories, governed directly by the Central Government. Examples include:

  • Delhi
  • Chandigarh
  • Puducherry
  • Ladakh
  • Andaman and Nicobar Islands

Popular Tourist Spots

  • Taj Mahal, Agra
  • Jaipur - The Pink City
  • Kerala Backwaters
  • Varanasi Ghats
  • Goa Beaches

Culture

India's culture is an amalgamation of art, architecture, music, dance, festivals, cuisine, and spirituality. Every region brings its own flavors, languages, and traditions to the mix.
